Why Migrate to Pixelfed?

What is Pixelfed?

Pixelfed is a free and decentralized alternative to Instagram. Based on the ActivityPub protocol, it's part of the "Fediverse" ‚ÅÇ - a network of independent servers that communicate with each other.

Key Benefits

Privacy First

Your data stays yours. No tracking, no mining, no selling to advertisers.

Freedom of Choice

Choose your server or host your own. You're not locked into one platform.
